    Issue 01 SS25 Seoul Words by NivelCrack

    Seoul in 1998 didn't look like a soccer city. But behind the neon and the concrete, a generation was raised on scratchy satellite broadcasts of Serie A, photocopied fanzines, and hand-painted kits from the Itaewon market. NivelCrack is a love letter to that version of the game — the one you had to work to see, the one that bled into street style before it was a category.
